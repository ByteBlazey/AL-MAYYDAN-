About Our Foundation

AL-MAYYDAN Islamic Foundation was established with a sacred mission: to nurture the next generation of Muslims through comprehensive Quranic education while serving our community with unwavering dedication. We believe in the transformative power of knowledge and the importance of giving back to those in need.

Quranic Education

We provide comprehensive Islamic education, focusing on teaching children to read, understand, and memorize the Holy Quran with proper Tajweed.

Community Service

We foster a strong sense of community, bringing families together in faith while promoting Islamic values and spiritual development.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To become a beacon of Islamic knowledge and community service, inspiring generations to live by the teachings of the Quran and the Sunnah of our beloved Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).

Mission: We are dedicated to providing high-quality Islamic education to children, ensuring they develop a strong foundation in Quranic recitation, memorization, and understanding. Simultaneously, we extend our services to the broader community through charitable programs, educational outreach, and social support for those facing hardship.
